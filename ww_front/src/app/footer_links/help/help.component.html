Help

Guests:

Unauthorized users a.k.a. guests are allowed to browse the webapp as long as they're not interacting with it directly. To be precise, the guests are allowed to do the following:

  • Browse the categories and products
  • Read the product details
  • Search for specific products
  • Read comments

Customers:

Authorized users a.k.a. customers are allowed to do exactly the same as the unauthorized users, except they're also allowed to interact with the webapp by making purchases or commenting, in short they are allowed to do the following:

  • Browse the categories and products
  • Read the product details
  • Search for specific products
  • Read comments
  • Add products to the basket
  • Choose a shipment method
  • Check out
  • Rate products
  • Leave comments on the product pages
  • Access user's purchase history
  • Edit preferences and personal information

Manufacturers:

Manufacturers are required to have their own official accounts through their trusted representatives. Such accounts are different in many ways to the ordinary customer accounts, and are allowed to do the following:

  • Browse the categories and products
  • Read the product details
  • Search for specific products
  • Read comments
  • Add new products
  • Alter details of the existing products
  • Leave comments on the product pages
  • Access sales history
  • Edit preferences and corporate information

How to become a supplier

To create a manufacturer account however you must follow a few more steps than an average user, namely:

  1. Special blanks must be filled out to request our partnership. To acquire these blanks contact the HR department.
  2. A meeting in person must be arranged between your company's legal representatives and our department of cooperation.
  3. After that a special commission will be sent to check the quality of your goods.
  4. If the goods are compliant with our standards as well as international standards of quality, such as RoHS, another meeting will be held this time with CEOs of both companies to arrange the conditions of our agreement.
  5. After the meeting, your official representatives will receive a special code for registration.
  6. If you have any questions regarding the process contact the HR department.
